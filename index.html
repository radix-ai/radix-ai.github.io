Who are we

At radix.ai, we design and build custom Machine Learning solutions that deliver quantifiable business value.

We hand-pick projects that we are genuinely interested in. We love projects where the latest advances in Deep Learning open doors to new applications or raise performance to unseen levels.

Our team has been carefully assembled so that we can maximize impact with a small and focused team. We value skill, motivation and a can-do attitude. We are looking for outstanding individuals who want to be part of this exceptional team.

What you’ll do

You will work with our team of Machine Learning and Software Engineers to design, implement, deploy, and maintain our Machine Learning solutions.

You will participate in cutting edge research in Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning applications.

You’ll have the chance to contribute to all aspects of an end-to-end Machine Learning solution. We’ll help you identify where you can, and want, to deliver the most value.

Who you are

You have a passion for Machine Learning and accelerating the world through technology.

You know your way around Python, the SciPy stack (pandas, numpy, scikit-learn), source control and reproducibility tooling (git, conda, docker), and have a basic understanding of the leading cloud providers (AWS, GCP, Azure). Knowledge of Deep Learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch) and parallel computing libraries (dask, numba) is a plus.

You can work autonomously and you deliver. You enjoy thoughtful conversations about problems and perfecting designs, but in the end you know that what matters is delivering a manufacturable solution that works every time.

You are excited to join an incredibly talented, and fun team that loves to take on new challenges. You believe in radix.ai’s mission to change the world with AI and want to be a core actor in pushing this envelope.

Our offer

We offer a competitive package with benefits, an inspiring and collaborative environment and the opportunity to work with, and learn from, the best in the field.
