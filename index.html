Who we are

radix .ai is a Belgium-based Machine Learning company.

Our mission is to accelerate the pace at which Machine Learning innovations are turned into reality.

We help big companies such as Atlas Copco and Brussels Airport, research institutes such as Imec, public companies such as VDAB, and startups .

Our core tools are Python, Scikit-learn, and TensorFlow, yet our team are full-stack engineers well versed in all technologies for building end-to-end Machine Learning solutions.

Machine Learning Engineer Read more

What you'll do

You will work with our team of engineers to design, implement and deploy end-to-end Machine Learning solutions for ourselves and our clients. You’ll have the opportunity to work on challenging and rewarding problems, including:

  • Building and deploying reproducible and scalable training pipelines.
  • Distributing training across GPUs and nodes with data and model parallelism.
  • Sequentially reading natural language with Deep Learning.
  • Developing Machine Learning-based search algorithms.
  • Scaling Machine Learning models to thousands of users.
  • Designing Machine Learning models to run on mobile devices.

Your qualifications

  • You are confident in your Software Engineering skills.
  • You are deeply interested in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
  • You have a master's or Ph.D. in Computer Science or a similar field.
  • You have delivered at least one real-life project.

Your technology stack

  • You have mastered the SciPy stack ( pandas, numpy, scikit-learn).
  • You know your way around source control and reproducibility tooling ( git, conda, pip, docker).
  • Experience with Deep Learning frameworks ( TensorFlow, PyTorch), and parallel computing libraries ( dask, numba) is a plus.
